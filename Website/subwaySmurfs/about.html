About Us

At Fuzzybottom Animal Hospital, we know your pet is a beloved member of your family — and we’re honored to be part of their care. Since 1993, Dr. Fuzzybottom has been proudly serving the Tri-Cities community, offering over 25 years of experience in small animal and emergency medicine. His deep understanding of veterinary science, combined with a genuine compassion for animals, ensures that every patient receives personalized, thoughtful care. Dr. Fuzzybottom believes that every pet is unique, and he approaches each case with a focus on the pet’s needs within the larger context of their family. At Fuzzybottom Animal Hospital, our dedicated team works closely with pet owners to deliver the highest standard of veterinary medicine, treating every pet with the respect, kindness, and expertise they deserve. Your pet’s health, happiness, and well-being are always our top priorities.
